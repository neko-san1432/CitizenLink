Privacy Notice

DRIMS is committed to upholding your right to privacy while delivering responsive public service.

DRIMS is operated by the Digos City Local Government Unit (LGU). We respect your privacy and process personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and its implementing rules.

This Privacy Notice describes how we handle your information when you create an account, file complaints, or interact with the platform.

What This Notice Covers

  • How DRIMS collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our services.
  • The choices available to you regarding the use of your information.
  • How you can exercise your rights under Philippine data protection laws.

Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to process your requests and improve public services:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, mobile number, email address (when you sign up).
  • Incident Data: Photos, videos, descriptions, and geolocation data of reported issues.
  • Device Information: browser type, IP address, and access times for security and analytics.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Verifying and resolving reported incidents.
  • Coordinating with LGU departments (e.g., Engineering, Health, Traffic).
  • Sending you status updates on your reports.
  • Urban planning analysis (using anonymized/aggregated data).

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is only shared with:

  • Authorized LGU departments relevant to your report.
  • Law enforcement agencies (only if required by law or in emergencies).

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@digos.gov.ph for assistance.
